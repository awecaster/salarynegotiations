Negotiations 101

Know Your Value

Often times, explaining what you bring to the table can be daunting. Think of the conversation as a sale. Is your counterpart aware of your true value? Expect to earn the higher end of the industry rate for your title.

Craft Your Argument

Prepare a document with your achievements. Know what your bottom line is and know what things are necessary for you to be more productive and satisfied in your role.

Practice

Pracitce waiting for your counterpart to call a number first. Practice asking for more than that number or for negotiating for benfits. Create the confidence you need to succed.
